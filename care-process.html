Recurring appointments for peace of mind

Once you’ve completed two sessions with a caregiver, you can make that schedule recurring right from the booking flow. Choose weekly, bi‑weekly, or monthly, pick the days, and lock in your routine—both you and your caregiver see it on your dashboards and live calendar.

  • Parents: toggle “Make this recurring” after two sessions, set cadence, days, and start date.
  • Caregivers: recurring bookings show clearly on your dashboard and calendar.
  • Everyone: adjust or cancel future occurrences with clear visibility for both sides.

Enjoy a consistent schedule and fewer back‑and‑forths, while keeping your family’s routine steady.

Learning Pathways & Curriculum

Families pick the focus areas, providers guide the sessions, and children share what they learned—all within a safe, tech-enabled experience.

Parent choice

Select curriculum tracks (prayer basics, Prophet stories, character, Arabic letters, kindness challenges) tailored to your child.

Provider-led, interactive

Caregivers use our guided content and activities during care time—hands-on practice, reflection prompts, and joyful storytelling.

Child portfolios

Kids showcase progress—reciting duas, sharing art, or summarizing stories—visible to parents in their accounts.

Feedback & praise loop

Parents can praise, leave notes, and suggest next focuses; providers see the feedback and adjust sessions accordingly.

Positive use of tech

We keep technology purposeful—simple dashboards, gentle reminders, and secure sharing—so learning feels warm, not screen-heavy.

At-home extensions

Between sessions, children keep practicing at their pace—dua and salah basics, kindness missions, story retells—while providers see progress and parents can praise in their dashboard.

Built-In Safety and Support

Every step includes identity verification, background checks, and values alignment. We offer CPR and First Aid guidance for providers and a responsive support team for families so both sides feel confident long after the first booking.
